Frequently Asked Questions

Farm Help is an online platform that provides a marketplace for farmers to buy and sell agricultural tools, rent farming equipment, and get advice via a chatbot.

To list your equipment for rent, simply go to the "Rental" section, where you can provide details about your equipment and set the rental terms.

You can browse and buy farming supplies directly from our marketplace. Simply visit the "Marketplace" section and explore the variety of seeds, tools, and other supplies available for purchase.

You can contact customer support by visiting the "Contact" section on our website, where you will find a contact form and other methods to get in touch with us.
